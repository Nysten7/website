Vocabulary for Ordering a Pizza

You'll find here all the terms and common phrases found when placing an order for a pizza, no matter the size, toppings, or store! (should only be used for Domino's though)

Pizza
A baked circle (or rectangle) of pizza dough, tomato sauce, and cheese. Coming from Italy, this staple for a meal has been appreciated around the world, with different styles being created (for example, New York style pizza)
Topping(s)
A food item which goes on top of a basic pizza. Popular toppings are peperoni, basil, mushrooms, and (unfortunatley) pineapple
Size
The size of the diameter is commonly used as a form of measurement for pizzas. Sometimes when pizza comes as slices, the actual size of the slice is relative to the store. The largeer the pizza, the greater the price.
Price
The cost of a pizza, paid in currency, exchange of goods/services, or not paid for at all (which is considered a crime)
Delivery Instructions
Instructions for where to deliver the pizza, as well as any special instrictions for preparing the pizza
Crimes Agaist Humanity
Ordering a pizza without cheese, sauce, and/or with pineapple are considered crimes agaist humanity and should be prosecuted to the full extent of the law
Delivery Person
The person who will deliver your pizza to your house/appartment. They will bring the pizza to the door if you select to pay in person, or they may drop it off on your doorstep if you request and have paid online
Tip
A nice way of saying thank you to your delivery person. If you are outside of America this is optional, pay whatever ammount you feel comfortable with. If you in America, 15-20% of the total cost of the meal
Pizza Box
The box the pizza comes in. Note: this box is NOT recyclable
Slice
A peice of pizza. If the pizza is a circle, the slice should be a triangle, and for rectangle pizzas rectangles. Any other shape is another crime agaist humanity
Crust
The bread of the pizza. Can be filled with cheese, and can be crunchy or soft
Family Sized Pizza
A pizza meant for an entire family, however, 12% of single Americans order this kind of pizza for themselves, either to be eaten in one sitting or multiple
