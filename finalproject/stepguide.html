Step by Step Guide on Ordering Pizza

  2. STEP TWO

    • Wait for your pizza to arrive
      • If you ar impatient, deal with it, then continue
      • If you are in a life or death situtation, add the cost and tip for the pizza to your will, then continue
  3. STEP THREE
    • Once you delivery person arrives at your door, open the door and accept your pizza
